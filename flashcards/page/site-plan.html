StudyFlashCards Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

StudyFlashCards is a study app where students can practice with digital flashcards.

Audience

This site is for high school and college students who need to review vocab, terms, and class concepts.

Dynamic elements

Users can flip each card, move to the next or previous card, shuffle the deck, and mark cards as known or review.

Content

Home

The Home page has a short welcome message, quick instructions, and a Start Studying button.

Images: the app logo and simple visuals.

Data shown: total number of cards in the deck.

Study Session page

This page shows one flashcard at a time with the question on the front and answer on the back. It includes buttons for Previous, Next, Shuffle, Known, and Review, plus a progress bar.

Sample data: { term: "Parallelism", definition: "Tasks run at the same time" }, { term: "Concurrency", definition: "Tasks make progress over the same time period" }.
